Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?
Yes, we offer free, no-obligation estimates for most of our projects. Simply contact us with the details of your task, and we will provide a transparent quote.
Are your technicians licensed and insured?
Absolutely. All our handymen and technicians undergo rigorous background checks, are fully licensed in their respective trades, and are fully insured for your peace of mind.
Do I need to supply the materials?
You can choose to supply your own materials if you prefer, or we can source high-quality materials for you. We discuss all material costs transparently before starting any work.
How quickly can you respond to an emergency?
We have a dedicated 24/7 emergency response team. Depending on your location within the Greater New York Area, we typically arrive within 1-2 hours for urgent issues like pipe bursts or electrical faults.
Do you guarantee your work?
Yes! We take pride in our workmanship. We offer a 100% satisfaction guarantee along with a standard warranty on labor and parts supplied by us.
